    1. BCA Department:

    About the Programme:

    Bachelor in Computer Application (B.C.A.) is an undergraduate degree course in computer applications. Day by day the demand for computer professionals is rapidly growing in the IT industry in India. This increasing demand growth of the IT industry has created a lot of opportunities for computer graduates as the application developer, web developer, mobile app developer, etc. Bachelor in Computer Application (B.C.A.) is one of the popular courses to make a career in the IT (Information Technology) field for the students. The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the subjects like a relational database, networking, data structure, core programming languages like ‘C’, ‘C++’, ‘Java’, and ‘Python’. It also includes Andriod development for mobile app development. This course provides a lot of opportunities for students who are interested in the computer field and wants to work in the IT sector as a programmer or software developer. Also after completion of this course student may go for higher studies like M.C.A., M.S., M.Sc., etc.

    2. MCA Department:

    About the Programme:

    Master of Computer Applications (M. C. A.) is a two-year (four semesters) professional Master's Degree in computer science and application. The course was designed to meet the growing demand for qualified professionals in the field of Information Technology. It is a postgraduate course that can be taken up after obtaining any Bachelor's Degree. The MCA program is inclined more toward Application Development and thus has more emphasis on the latest programming language and tools to develop better and faster applications. It covers various aspects of computational theory, programming, algorithm design and optimization, network and relational database management, mobile technologies, and the latest subjects like data science, machine learning, etc. The MCA program prepares students to take up positions as system analysts, systems designers, data analysts, data scientist, web developer, Mobile app developer, programmers, and managers in any field related to information technology. The program, therefore, aims at imparting comprehensive knowledge with equal emphasis on theory and practice. After the completion of the course student may go for higher studies like Ph. D.

    3. CSE Department

    Department Of Computer Science and Engineering

    Computer Science and Engineering Department is established & approved by AICET, New Delhi in the year 2009-10, with an aim of building creative IT professionals to acquire core knowledge and skills required by IT industry or research institutes to meet global challenges. The courses are taught by the industry experts, experienced, qualified faculty and the global researchers. The laboratories are set up with open source softwares, high configuration computer systems for technical training and research work. Regular knowledge sharing sessions are conducted by experts and alumni to keep up with the rapidly evolving technology.

    Programme Offered:-

    UG Programme: B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering
    PG Programme: M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering
    Ph.D.: Ph. D in Computer Science & Engineering
