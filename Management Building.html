Commerce & Management Building


School of Commerce and Management (SCM) is committed towards creating, preserving and imparting internationally benchmarked knowledge & skills to a diverse community of students. SCM provides a variety of opportunities for students to improve their employability. SCM capitalizes on the industry and academic experience of its faculty to prepare students to work effectively in today's complex and challenging business environment. SCM offers undergraduate commerce courses (B.Com), postgraduate commerce courses (M. Com.), undergraduate (B.B.A.), postgraduate (M.B.A.). The commerce and management offer UG and PG programs in and management, which are industry oriented with a right mix of knowledge in areas of Management, Commerce and Finance. The objective of management courses undergraduate (B.B.A.), postgraduate (M.B.A.) and commerce programs offered by SCM is to enable students to embark upon a management career.

Department Of Commerce

Commerce program is unique with emphasis on case based learning, state of the art infrastructure, emphasis on acquiring practical and life skills, establishing long and lasting Institute-Industry interface culminating in paving successful careers for all Commerce graduates.
