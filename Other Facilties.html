  3. Hostel












    4. SGU has hostel facilities equipped with modern amenities separate for boys and girls with well furnished room accommodating three students each. There are 7 blocks of hostels in the campus. The rooms have individual bed, study table, books shelve, and cupboards. The hostels are provided with Wi-Fi access free of cost,stationary shops, copier facilities, mini super market and self laundry facility. To ensure safet of the students each hostel has separate rectors with security services. The hostel blocks are under surveillance of CCTV.
    Dining: There is a specious dining hall located centrally in Food Court. The food served is tasty and hygienic. Jain food can be availed at request. Food Court consisting of various stalls offers vegetarian food at reasonable prices. There are fast food outlets attached to the Hostel apart from canteen facility.
    Health Facility: Primary health care facility is available at SGU Health Care Centre. It provides OPD services from 9.00am. to 5.30pm. on all University working days. The total dependent population is about 5,000 people which include students, employees and their authorized dependents, retired employees & their dependents and the various non-entitled people like ex-students, servant quarters residents, sanitary staff, security staff, house-keeping staff, various projects staff, working in the University.
    Other Amenities: Regular Banking and ATM services are available in the SGU premises. For the comfort of guest and parents, the guest house at SGU campus offers fully furnished rooms. The vast indoor and outdoor play fields are available. Each hostel block is facilitated with Television set.
